HOW TO PLAY

☀️
Morning

Sell cards to rivals. Claim honest — or bluff a pricier one for coins.

🌤️
Afternoon

Supply raid — grab 3 of 6 castle rooms before the timer ends. Choose wisely!

🌅
Evening

The chosen cook serves clean (+3🪙) or poisons everyone else.

🌙
Midnight

Hide assassins, then pick a bed. Each assassin hits for 2 HP.

⚔️
Challenge

Catch a bluff: liar loses 2🪙. Wrongly accuse: you lose 2🪙.
