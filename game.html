HOW TO PLAY

🎯
Goal

Outlast your rivals over the nights — be the last ruler standing. Most games run about 5 days.

☀️
Day — trade

Take turns. Sell character cards to rivals: an HONEST sale pays you a +2🪙 bonus, or bluff a pricier one to scam them.

🧔
Merchant

Play it free to BUY a rival's item with coins. Use 🧪/💰 items on yourself; aim ☠️ Poison at a rival.

🏪
Market & 🗺️ Map

Open any time during the day (pauses play). Market: spend coins on buffs, assassins, rare cards. Map: hide your 🥷 assassins in rooms.

🌙
Night

Choose where YOU sleep. Assassins hidden in your room hit for 2 HP each. Then dawn — and poison — arrives.

⚔️
Challenge

Catch a bluff: liar loses coins. Wrongly accuse an honest seller: you lose 2🪙.

✨ UPDATE ✨

WHAT'S NEW

The castle has changed — here's what's fresh.

⚡
Streamlined Flow

No more afternoon raid or cooking feast — a smooth loop: trade by day, sleep at night, repeat.

🤝
Honesty Pays

Selling a character card honestly earns the seller a +2🪙 bonus on top of the price.

🏪
Market & Map Anytime

Open the Market or Castle Map whenever you like — they pause the game so you act on your schedule.

☠️
Poison Vial

Poison a rival for 2 HP at next dawn. The Witch shrugs it off — clearly shown.

🃏
Jester · 🧙 Wizard · 🌿 Druid

Jester swaps hands on a clean bluff, Wizard strikes −2/+1, Druid grants +1 max HP. Bots use items too!

⚖️
Rebalanced

Bigger life: everyone starts at 10 HP, the Giant at 15, with fuller starting hands.
